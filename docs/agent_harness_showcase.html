SHEET 08

When it earned its keep

Two moments the rails actually mattered — both preserved on this drawing.
D16 · the judge pivot

The conclusion moved pairs — honestly.

A 13B judge disagreed with my first regex scorer and relocated the study's only significant safety regression from Qwen-4B (+0.065) to Qwen-1.7B (+0.055, p=0.027). Because raw outputs are immutable, nothing was re-run — the fix was a new sidecar.

D21 · it caught itself

The harness failed its own CI — and emailed me.

The immutability check I built to guard 36 evidence files ran in CI, where those gitignored files can't exist — so it failed every push and emailed the owner. The fix taught the gate the right rule: absence is not mutation.

“The conclusion changed twice. The raw evidence changed zero times.”
SHEET 09

The principle

Not a smarter prompt.
